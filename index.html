Our process

Here’s how we put in place a solution that meets your specific business needs, not just a one-size-fits-all approach
A
B
1. Detailed planning
Before a line of code is written, we’ll work with you to put together a detailed plan describing the project in detail (without technical jargon), setting out the estimated timetable, project milestones, fixed costs, and responsibilities
2. Regular progress
Features are released little and often rather than all at once, giving you plenty of time to get used to using them. By starting with the most important/essential features, this also means we can get to initial launch quicker: in some cases 30 days
3. Training
We will provide training throughout the project – ensuring launch is a low-effort, high-impact event with staff ready to use from day one. Training is incorporated into a training guide for current or future colleagues
4. Weekly reports
Every week you’ll get a weekly report that covers what has been done, what is next, and what is blocked (with what is needed from a named stakeholder to resolve this)
5. Ongoing support
Notice something wrong? There is never any charge for warranty work. Pay-as-you-go-support means we’re there when needed (though you shouldn’t need much with the training provided). Got new requirements? We can simply and quickly make this happen for you any time after initial launch
